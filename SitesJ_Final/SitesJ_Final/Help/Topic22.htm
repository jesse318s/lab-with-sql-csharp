The cart contains a list of your games selecte for purchase. Click on the text for the ID or title of the game you wish to remove, and click "Remove Item" to remove the game. You may also click the "Clear Cart" button to remove all games from your cart. You may also fill out your name in the boxes labeled "First Name" and "Last Name" for your receipt. Then, by clicking the "Check Out" button you may purchase your cart items and you will receive your receipt as a report in a new window. You may exit the cart by clicking "Continue Shopping".
